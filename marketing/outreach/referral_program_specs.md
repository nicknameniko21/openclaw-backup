# Referral Program Specifications

## REFERRAL PROGRAM OVERVIEW

### Core Philosophy
Based on Cialdini's Reciprocity Principle and the Dropbox/Uber referral model: **Both referrer and referee benefit**, creating a win-win that motivates sharing.

### Program Goals
1. **Acquisition:** 20% of new customers from referrals within 12 months
2. **Retention:** Referred customers have 2x higher LTV
3. **Viral Coefficient:** Target K-factor > 1.0 (each user brings 1+ new user)
4. **Cost Efficiency:** CAC via referral 50% lower than paid channels

## REFERRAL PROGRAM METRICS & TRACKING

### Key Metrics

#### Acquisition Metrics
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Referral Share Rate | 30% of users share | Users with 1+ share / Total users |
| Referral Click Rate | 20% of shares clicked | Clicks / Shares |
| Referral Conversion Rate | 10% of clicks convert | Signups / Clicks |
| Viral Coefficient (K) | >1.0 | New users from referrals / Total users |

#### Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Active Referrers | 15% of user base | Users with 1+ successful referral |
| Referrals per User | 2.0 | Total referrals / Active referrers |
| Time to First Referral | <30 days | Days from signup to first share |

#### Business Metrics
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Referral CAC | 50% of paid CAC | Cost of rewards / Referral customers |
| Referral LTV | 2x non-referral | LTV of referred customers |
| Referral % of Total | 20% of new customers | Referral signups / Total signups |

### Tracking Implementation

#### Technical Requirements
1. **Referral attribution:** UTM parameters + cookie tracking
2. **Dashboard:** Real-time referral stats for users
3. **Admin panel:** Program-wide metrics and fraud detection
4. **Automation:** Credit application, email notifications

#### Fraud Prevention
- Duplicate account detection
- Self-referral blocking
- Unusual pattern flagging
- Manual review for large redemptions
