## EMAIL DELIVERY BEST PRACTICES

### Technical Setup
- **SPF, DKIM, DMARC:** Configure for your domain
- **Warm-up:** Start with 10-20 emails/day, ramp up over 2-4 weeks
- **Reputation monitoring:** Use tools like MailTester, GlockApps
- **List hygiene:** Verify emails before sending (NeverBounce, ZeroBounce)

### Sending Strategy
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM recipient's timezone
- **Volume:** Max 50-100 emails/day per sender
- **Personalization:** At minimum, customize first name, company, and one specific detail

### Tracking
- **Open rates:** 40-60% is good for cold email
- **Reply rates:** 5-15% is good for cold email
- **Meeting booking rate:** 1-5% is typical
- **A/B test:** Subject lines, opening lines, CTAs

### Compliance
- **CAN-SPAM:** Include physical address, unsubscribe link
- **GDPR:** Only email business addresses, provide opt-out
- **CASL:** Implied consent for B2B, but be respectful
