## Product Hunt Launch Schedule (Launch Day)

### Pre-Launch (12:00 AM - 12:01 AM PST)

**12:00 AM PST:**
- [ ] Product Hunt listing goes live
- [ ] Confirm listing is correct (images, copy, links)
- [ ] Post first maker comment
- [ ] Send "We're live!" message to team

### Early Morning (12:01 AM - 6:00 AM PST)

**12:15 AM:**
- [ ] Share PH link in company Slack/Discord
- [ ] Post on personal Twitter/X accounts
- [ ] Send first email to waitlist ("We're live on Product Hunt!")

**2:00 AM:**
- [ ] Check initial traction
- [ ] Respond to first comments
- [ ] Share in relevant Slack communities (be respectful of rules)

**4:00 AM:**
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (founder accounts)
- [ ] Engage with early upvoters
- [ ] Monitor for any technical issues

**6:00 AM:**
- [ ] Morning team standup (virtual)
- [ ] Review overnight metrics
- [ ] Adjust strategy if needed

### Morning Push (6:00 AM - 12:00 PM PST)

**7:00 AM:**
- [ ] Send email to full list (not just waitlist)
- [ ] Post on company social accounts
- [ ] Engage actively in PH comments

**8:00 AM:**
- [ ] Share in relevant Reddit communities (r/SaaS, r/entrepreneur, etc.)
- [ ] Reach out to friendly accounts for support
- [ ] Post update/story on PH (if feature available)

**9:00 AM:**
- [ ] LinkedIn company post
- [ ] Instagram story (if applicable)
- [ ] Respond to all PH comments within 15 minutes

**10:00 AM:**
- [ ] Check leaderboard position
- [ ] Send personalized messages to key supporters
- [ ] Post "behind the scenes" content

**11:00 AM:**
- [ ] Mid-morning social post
- [ ] Review and respond to all new comments
- [ ] Monitor conversion rates

### Afternoon Sustain (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM PST)

**12:00 PM:**
- [ ] Lunch break (rotate team)
- [ ] Post lunch update on social
- [ ] Check analytics dashboard

**1:00 PM:**
- [ ] Share user testimonials/reviews
- [ ] Engage with PH hunters/makers
- [ ] Post in relevant Facebook groups

**2:00 PM:**
- [ ] Afternoon email (different angle)
- [ ] Twitter/X thread about launch journey
- [ ] Respond to all comments/questions

**3:00 PM:**
- [ ] Share milestone ("Top 5!" or similar)
- [ ] Thank supporters publicly
- [ ] Post demo video/tutorial

**4:00 PM:**
- [ ] Check server performance
- [ ] Engage with late-day PH visitors
- [ ] Share customer success story

**5:00 PM:**
- [ ] Begin planning "last push" messaging
- [ ] Review day's learnings
- [ ] Prepare evening content

### Evening Push (6:00 PM - 12:00 AM PST)

**6:00 PM:**
- [ ] "Final hours" email to list
- [ ] Social posts about final push
- [ ] Engage with evening PH traffic

**7:00 PM:**
- [ ] Share day 1 metrics (if impressive)
- [ ] Thank community supporters
- [ ] Post on evening-active platforms

**8:00 PM:**
- [ ] Monitor for any last-minute issues
- [ ] Continue engaging with comments
- [ ] Prepare day 2 strategy

**9:00 PM:**
- [ ] Team debrief (what worked, what didn't)
- [ ] Schedule day 2 content
- [ ] Rest rotation begins

**10:00 PM - 12:00 AM:**

- [ ] Minimal engagement
- [ ] Prepare for day 2

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |

| Server crash | Low | High | Load testing, auto-scaling, CDN |
| PH listing rejected | Low | High | Follow guidelines, have backup plan |
| Negative comments | Medium | Medium | Professional responses, don't argue |
| Low initial traction | Medium | High | Warm up audience, have backup influencers |
| Technical bugs | Medium | High | Extensive QA, rollback plan |
| Team burnout | Medium | Medium | Rotate shifts, celebrate wins |
