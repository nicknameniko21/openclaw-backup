### Moderation Policy

**Strike System:**
- **1st Violation:** Warning (DM + log)
- **2nd Violation:** 24-hour timeout
- **3rd Violation:** 7-day timeout
- **4th Violation:** Permanent ban

**Immediate Ban Offenses:**
- Hate speech or harassment
- Sharing illegal content
- Doxxing or privacy violations
- Spam/scam attempts
- Intentional misinformation

**Moderator Guidelines:**
- Document all actions in mod-log
- Be consistent with enforcement
- Assume good intent initially
- Escalate uncertain situations

## Engagement Tactics

### Daily Engagement

**Morning Kickoff (9 AM ET):**
- Bot posts "Question of the Day" in #general-chat
- Topics rotate: product tips, industry news, fun questions
- Example: "What's your biggest meeting pet peeve? ğŸ“"

**Office Hours (Weekly):**
- 1-hour voice channel session
- Product team answers questions
- Rotating topics (API deep dive, use case spotlight, etc.)
- Recorded and posted to #tutorials

### Weekly Engagement

**Monday: Feature Spotlight**
- Highlight one feature with tips
- Ask for feedback
- Share customer success story

**Wednesday: Winsday**
- Members share recent wins
- Could be product-related or personal
- Celebrates community achievements

**Friday: Show and Tell**
- Members share their setups
- Screenshots of dashboards welcome
- Best share gets "Community Star" role for the week

### Monthly Engagement

**Community AMA (Ask Me Anything):**
- Guest: Founder, CTO, or notable customer
- 1-hour text-based AMA
- Promoted 1 week in advance
- Transcript posted after

**Product Update Livestream:**
- Voice channel presentation
- New features demo
- Q&A session
- Recording shared in #announcements

**Community Challenge:**
- Monthly themed challenge
- Examples:
  - "Best automation workflow" (Meetily Pro)
  - "Most creative verification use case" (AI Identity Pro)
  - "Best integration setup"
- Winner gets swag or account credit

### Quarterly Engagement

**Community Survey:**
- Product feedback
- Community satisfaction
- Feature prioritization vote
- Results shared transparently

**Virtual Meetup:**
- Zoom/Google Meet event
- Networking + product updates
- Customer spotlight presentations
- Recording for those who can't attend

**Power User Recognition:**
- Top 10 contributors recognized
- Special "Power User" badge
- Early access to beta features
- Direct line to product team

## Community Team Structure

### Roles Needed:

**Community Manager (1):**
- Overall strategy and execution
- Content calendar management
- Partner with product/marketing
- Report on metrics

**Moderators (2-3):**
- Day-to-day moderation
- Support escalation
- Event facilitation
- Member engagement

**Product Liaisons (2):**
- Technical question support
- Feature request triage
- Beta testing coordination
- Documentation updates

### Time Commitment:
- Community Manager: 20-30 hrs/week
- Moderators: 5-10 hrs/week each
- Product Liaisons: 2-5 hrs/week each
