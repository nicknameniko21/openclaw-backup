## Partnership Outreach Email Template

### Initial Outreach

**Subject:** Partnership opportunity: [Their Company] + [Our Company]

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

I'm [Your Name], [Title] at [Company]. We build [brief value prop].

I've been following [Their Company] and love what you're doing with [specific feature/achievement]. Our products serve similar customers in the [industry] space, and I see a natural integration opportunity.

Here's the idea:
[One sentence describing the integration and mutual benefit]

For example, [Similar Partner] integrated with us and saw [specific result]. Their customers now [benefit description].

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to explore this? I think there's real potential for a win-win partnership.

[Your Name]
[Title]
[Company]
[Calendar Link]

P.S. - Happy to share our integration documentation upfront if you'd like to evaluate the technical fit first.
```

### Follow-Up (1 week later)

**Subject:** Re: Partnership opportunity

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Wanted to follow up on my note about a potential partnership between [Their Company] and [Company].

I know partnerships can be a big commitment, so I wanted to suggest a low-risk way to test this:

[Specific proposal - e.g., co-hosted webinar, joint case study, simple API integration]

This would let us:
• Validate customer interest
• Generate leads for both of us
• Build the foundation for deeper integration

Worth a brief conversation?

[Your Name]
```

### Final Follow-Up (2 weeks later)

**Subject:** Should I close the loop on this?

**Body:**
```
Hi [First Name],

I don't want to keep following up if partnerships aren't a priority right now.

Should I close the loop on this, or is there a better time to revisit?

Totally understand if now isn't right. I'll keep following [Their Company]'s progress either way.

[Your Name]
```

## Affiliate/Referral Program Structure

### Program Overview

**Program Name:** [Company] Partner Network

**Target Partners:**
- Consultants & agencies serving our target market
- Complementary SaaS companies
- Industry influencers & content creators
- Existing customers (customer referral program)

### Tier Structure

#### Tier 1: Affiliate (Entry Level)
**Requirements:** Apply and be approved
**Commission:** 20% recurring for 12 months
**Benefits:**
- Unique referral link
- Marketing materials access
- Monthly performance reports
- Standard support

#### Tier 2: Partner (Active Referrers)
**Requirements:** 5+ qualified referrals or $1K+ MRR generated
**Commission:** 25% recurring for 12 months
**Benefits:**
- Everything in Affiliate, plus:
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Early access to new features
- Dedicated partner manager
- Quarterly business reviews

#### Tier 3: Strategic Partner (High Volume)
**Requirements:** 25+ qualified referrals or $5K+ MRR generated
**Commission:** 30% recurring for 12 months
**Benefits:**
- Everything in Partner, plus:
- Custom integration support
- Joint case studies
- Speaking opportunities
- Annual partner summit invitation
- White-glove onboarding for referred customers

## Co-Marketing Opportunities

### Joint Webinars

**Format:** 45-minute educational webinar
**Responsibilities:**
- Each partner promotes to their audience
- Shared lead list (opt-in basis)
- Co-branded slides and materials
- Recording shared for future use

**Topics:**
- "The Future of Fintech Compliance" (with Vanta/Drata)
- "Building Secure Customer Onboarding" (with Auth0)
- "Remote Team Productivity Stack" (with Slack/Notion)

### Co-Authored Content

**Ebooks/Guides:**
- "The Complete Guide to Digital Identity Verification" (with compliance partner)
- "The Modern Meeting Playbook" (with collaboration partner)
- "Fintech Security Stack 2025" (with security partners)

**Blog Series:**
- Guest posts on partner blogs
- Cross-promotion in newsletters
- Social media collaboration

### Integration Marketplace Listings

**Strategy:**
- Prioritize high-traffic marketplaces (Salesforce AppExchange, HubSpot Marketplace)
- Optimize listings for search
- Collect and showcase reviews
- Regular updates with new features

### Joint Case Studies

**Format:**
- Customer success story featuring both products
- Metrics and ROI for both solutions
- Co-branded PDF and landing page
- Promotion by both partners
