## Content Best Practices

### Posting Schedule
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM EST
- **Frequency:** 3-4x per week per company page
- **Mix:** 40% educational, 30% social proof, 20% engagement, 10% promotional

### Engagement Strategy
1. Respond to all comments within 2 hours
2. Ask follow-up questions to drive conversation
3. Tag relevant people when appropriate
4. Share employee posts to company page
5. Engage with target audience's content (10-15 min/day)

### Hashtag Strategy
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags per post
- Mix popular (#SaaS, #AI) with niche (#IdentityVerification, #MeetingProductivity)
- Create branded hashtag: #AIIdentityPro #MeetilyPro

### Visual Guidelines
- Use consistent brand colors
- Include logo on all graphics
- Optimize for mobile (square or 4:5 ratio)
- Add alt text for accessibility
