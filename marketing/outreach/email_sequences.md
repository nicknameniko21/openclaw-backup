## Email Best Practices

### Timing
- **Send Days:** Tuesday-Thursday perform best
- **Send Times:** 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM recipient's local time
- **Avoid:** Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend mode)

### Personalization
- Always use first name
- Reference specific company news/recent events
- Mention similar companies in their space
- Customize based on role/persona

### Length
- Keep under 125 words for best response rates
- Mobile-friendly formatting (short paragraphs)
- One clear CTA per email

### Tracking
- Open rates (target: >40%)
- Reply rates (target: >10%)
- Meeting booking rate (target: >5%)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep <2%)

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Subject line length (short vs. descriptive)
- CTA placement (early vs. end)
- Social proof type (metrics vs. quotes)
- P.S. usage (with vs. without)
