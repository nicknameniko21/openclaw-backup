## 3. LAUNCH DAY CHECKLIST

### 30 Days Before Launch
- [ ] Finalize product positioning and one-liner
- [ ] Create Product Hunt account (if new)
- [ ] Engage with community (comment on 5+ hunts/day)
- [ ] Reach out to 10+ potential hunters
- [ ] Prepare gallery images (5-7 high-quality screenshots/GIFs)
- [ ] Draft maker comment (first comment on launch)
- [ ] Write FAQ responses for common questions
- [ ] Set up exclusive launch offer/discount code
- [ ] Create "Coming Soon" teaser post
- [ ] Build email list of supporters to notify

### 7 Days Before Launch
- [ ] Confirm hunter and share preview access
- [ ] Schedule launch for 12:01 AM PT (optimal timing)
- [ ] Prepare social media content calendar
- [ ] Draft announcement posts for all channels
- [ ] Set up tracking (UTM parameters, analytics)
- [ ] Prepare press kit for media outreach
- [ ] Brief team on response protocols
- [ ] Test product thoroughly (no bugs on launch day!)

### 1 Day Before Launch
- [ ] Send reminder to hunter
- [ ] Notify email list ("Launching tomorrow!")
- [ ] Schedule social posts for launch morning
- [ ] Prepare live demo/video if applicable
- [ ] Set up real-time notifications for comments
- [ ] Ensure team availability for support
- [ ] Prepare "thank you" messages for supporters

### Launch Day (Hour by Hour)

#### 12:00 AM PT (Launch)
- [ ] Confirm product is live on PH
- [ ] Post maker comment immediately
- [ ] Share on Twitter, LinkedIn, all social channels
- [ ] Send email to list
- [ ] Notify Slack communities, relevant groups

#### 6:00 AM PT (Morning Push)
- [ ] Post morning update on social
- [ ] Respond to all overnight comments
- [ ] Reach out to personal network for support
- [ ] Share in relevant Reddit communities

#### 9:00 AM PT (Peak Hours Begin)
- [ ] Monitor comments closely, respond within 15 minutes
- [ ] Share updates/stats if trending
- [ ] Post in relevant online communities
- [ ] Engage with other products (be a good citizen)

#### 12:00 PM PT (Midday Check)
- [ ] Lunch break for team (stay fresh!)
- [ ] Post midday update
- [ ] Continue comment engagement
- [ ] Monitor competitor activity

#### 3:00 PM PT (Afternoon Push)
- [ ] Final social push
- [ ] Personal outreach to key contacts
- [ ] Respond to all pending comments
- [ ] Prepare end-of-day summary

#### 6:00 PM PT (Evening)
- [ ] Thank supporters publicly
- [ ] Continue monitoring comments
- [ ] Plan next day's follow-up

### Post-Launch (Day 2-7)
- [ ] Thank hunter personally
- [ ] Follow up with all commenters
- [ ] Analyze traffic and conversion data
- [ ] Reach out to press who covered launch
- [ ] Convert PH traffic (email capture, trials)
- [ ] Document lessons learned
- [ ] Plan next launch (updates, new products)

## 5. UPVOTE STRATEGY (PH Rules Compliant)

### âš ï¸ CRITICAL: Product Hunt Rules
- **DO NOT:** Ask for upvotes directly
- **DO NOT:** Use upvote exchanges or rings
- **DO NOT:** Create fake accounts
- **DO NOT:** Offer incentives for upvotes
- **DO:** Ask for feedback, comments, and engagement
- **DO:** Share authentically with your network

### Compliant Engagement Strategy

#### What to Ask For (Allowed)
```
"We just launched on Product Hunt! Would love your feedback and thoughts."

"Check out our Product Hunt launch and let us know what you think!"

"Join the discussion on our PH launchâ€”your input would mean a lot."
```

#### What NOT to Ask For (Against Rules)
```
âŒ "Please upvote us on Product Hunt"
âŒ "Help us get to #1 by upvoting"
âŒ "Upvote for upvote?"
âŒ "Get us 100 upvotes today"
```

### Pre-Launch Network Building
1. **Engage authentically** on Product Hunt for 30+ days before launch
2. **Build genuine relationships** with makers and hunters
3. **Provide value** in comments (not just "cool product!")
4. **Create a support list** of people who genuinely like your product

### Community Sharing (Where Allowed)
- **Indie Hackers:** Share in "Product Launch" section
- **Reddit:** r/SideProject, r/alphaandbetausers (follow sub rules)
- **Slack Communities:** Only where launches are welcome
- **Discord Servers:** Product-focused communities
- **Newsletter Swaps:** Partner with complementary newsletters

### Engagement Quality Over Quantity
- **Respond to every comment** within 15 minutes during peak hours
- **Ask follow-up questions** to drive deeper engagement
- **Share insights** not available on the landing page
- **Be human** - not corporate speak

### Tracking Metrics
- **Upvotes:** Track but don't obsess
- **Comments:** Quality of discussion matters more than count
- **Traffic:** UTM-tagged links to measure PH ROI
- **Conversions:** Signups, trials, purchases from PH traffic
- **Rank:** Product of the Day, Week, Month
