# LinkedIn Outreach Strategy

## Target Personas

### AI Identity Pro

**Primary Personas:**

1. **CTOs (Chief Technology Officers)**
   - Pain Points: Integration complexity, security concerns, scaling challenges
   - Goals: Reduce fraud, improve user experience, maintain compliance
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Active posters, engage with tech content, follow industry trends
   - Best Time to Reach: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 5-7 PM

2. **Compliance Officers**
   - Pain Points: Regulatory requirements, audit preparation, risk management
   - Goals: Ensure KYC/AML compliance, reduce fraud exposure, maintain audit trails
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Engage with compliance content, regulatory updates
   - Best Time to Reach: Monday-Wednesday, 9-11 AM

3. **Fintech Founders**
   - Pain Points: Limited resources, need to move fast, investor expectations
   - Goals: Launch quickly, build trust, reduce fraud costs
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Highly active, share startup journey, seek partnerships
   - Best Time to Reach: Daily, early morning or late evening

### Meetily Pro

**Primary Personas:**

1. **Project Managers**
   - Pain Points: Meeting inefficiency, action item tracking, team alignment
   - Goals: Run effective meetings, ensure follow-through, improve productivity
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Engage with productivity content, PM methodologies
   - Best Time to Reach: Tuesday-Thursday, 12-2 PM or 4-6 PM

2. **Executives (VP, C-Suite)**
   - Pain Points: Information overload, decision-making bottlenecks, time scarcity
   - Goals: Maximize meeting ROI, stay informed, drive accountability
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Thought leadership content, industry insights
   - Best Time to Reach: Early morning (7-9 AM) or Sunday evening

3. **Remote Team Leads**
   - Pain Points: Async communication, timezone coordination, team cohesion
   - Goals: Keep distributed teams aligned, reduce meeting fatigue
   - LinkedIn Behavior: Remote work content, team culture posts
   - Best Time to Reach: Flexible, often check LinkedIn throughout day

## Content Best Practices

### Posting Schedule
- **Optimal Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM local time
- **Frequency:** 1 post per day maximum
- **Weekend Posts:** Light engagement content only

### Engagement Strategy
- **Respond to Comments:** Within 2 hours during business hours
- **Engage with Others:** Spend 15 min/day commenting on target personas' posts
- **Tag Relevant People:** When genuinely relevant (don't spam)
- **Use Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags per post

### Content Mix (80/20 Rule)
- **80% Value:** Educational, entertaining, inspiring content
- **20% Promotional:** Product mentions, CTAs, sales content

### Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** Clear value proposition + keywords
- **Banner:** Product visual or company branding
- **About:** Story-driven, benefit-focused
- **Featured:** Case studies, free resources, product demos
